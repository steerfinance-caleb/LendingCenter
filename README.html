LendingCenter

LendingCenter is the platform designed, built, and used in the day to day operations of Vinter Auto Finance, LLC, a division of Steer Finance Holdings LLC.

Features:

Features include:

  • Applications from licensed dealers
  • Automated underwriting based on an exclusive algorhitm
  • Automated approval generation
  • Loan Acceptance from Customer

Licensing:

LendingCenter must be licensed by Steer Finance Holdings, LLC for any commercial use.
